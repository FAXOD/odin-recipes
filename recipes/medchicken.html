Mediterranean Chicken & Orzo

Description:

This hearty One Pot Mediterranean Chicken Orzo is a great slimming friendly meal whether you're counting calories or following a diet plan like Weight Watchers.

Ingredients

  • 6 Boneless Chicken Thighs
  • 250g Orzo
  • 1 Stick of Celery
  • 1 Carrot - Diced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 onion - diced
  • 1.5 tsp paprika
  • 4 mushrooms - sliced
  • 6 whole garlic cloves
  • 1 handful cherry tomatoes
  • 1 squirt lemon juice
  • 500ml chicken stock
  • 1 handful parsley leaves
  • 1.5 tsp allspice
  • 0.5 tsp tumeric
  • low calorie cooking spray

Steps:

  1. Season thighs and leave for 10 mins. Chop veg.
  2. Spray pan and heat gently.
  3. Cook thighs until brown on all sides.
  4. Set chicken aside.
  5. Spray the pan more. Fry onion, carrot, celery, garlic and mushrooms.
  6. Add tomatoes, lemon juice and 100ml of chicken stock.
  7. Add chicken to pan. Cover with foil and cook in oven at 180C for 30 mins.
  8. Remove pan, add orzo, parsley and stock. Return to oven for 20 mins.
  9. Serve.
