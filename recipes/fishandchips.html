Fish and Chips

Description:

A Northern-English staple, this is a simple recipe to cook fish, chips, broccoli & gravy. This recipe will use an air fryer, an oven and a hob and will take approx. 30 minutes to prepare. Serves 2.

Ingredients:

  • 2x Birds-Eye Frozen Cod
  • 4x Large Potatoes
  • 50g Broccoli
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Gravy Granules
  • Spray oil

Steps:

  1. Fill and boil the kettle. While boiling, peel and cut potatoes into chips.
  2. Begin preheating oven to 220C. Boil chips for 5 minutes.
  3. Place chips in air fryer for 25 minutes with light spraying of oil and some salt and pepper.
  4. Place fish in oven for 25 minutes.
  5. After 10 minutes, boil the broccoli. Once water is bubbling, leave simmering until needed.
  6. After 10 minutes, make the necessary amount of gravy and set to one side.
  7. Take fish, chips and broccoli and set on a plate. Add salt and pepper if needed. Add gravy and serve.
